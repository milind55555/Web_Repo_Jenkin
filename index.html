About FreshFields

We started as a small group of farmers and food lovers wanting to make fresh, healthy food easy to access. We partner with small family farms and prioritize sustainable practices.

Our mission: reduce food miles, support local agriculture, and deliver quality. We believe in full transparency — every box includes the farm name and harvesting date.
